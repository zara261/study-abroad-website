How to Apply: Basic Requirements

There are three intakes in the USA, Spring, Summer, and Fall terms. Most countries have only one or two terms, but the US has a unique system. The Fall semester begins in late August or early September whereas the Spring semester begins in early January. The Summer semester may start in May but it is only valid for limited courses and universities. International students are required to attach their English proficiency test scores with applications, such as TOEFL or IELTS. Some universities require specialized test scores like SAT or ACT. If the student applies for graduate school then GRE or GMT additional test scores are mandatory to attach with the application.
