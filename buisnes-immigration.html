Buisness immigration

Orbit Counsels specialises in assisting businesses, entrepreneurs and particularly skilled workers with their immigration aspects abroad. This includes assistance in complex visa applications (Start-up/Investor Visa, Entrepreneur Visa, Citizenship by Investment) obtaining work permits, compliance with immigration laws and advising on ancillary matters covering the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, EU and the rest of the world.

Our services aim to advise individuals with suitable options and routes for gaining visas, work permits, permanent residency or citizenship based on their exceptional skills, qualifications and circumstances.

Furthermore, we advise businesses keen to expand globally by setting up a branch office, moving/offshoring or considering market prospects in another country while ensuring compliance with immigration regulations. We also offer visit visa services for businesses and trade delegations seeking travel approval for crucial business meetings, conferences, trade fairs and events abroad with the intention to streamline processes involved in individual/group travel abroad. By utilising our services, entrepreneurs and skilled professionals can increase their chances of a successful immigration process and achieve their desired goals of living and working in a new country. The entire process is handled with thorough professionalism ensuring a convenient and efficient experience for our clients.
