Study Visa

Orbit Counsels At Orbit Counsels, we are focused on providing student visa services that are of invaluable support to international students navigating the complex process of applying for and obtaining a student visa for education purposes.

We possess the expertise in handling immigration requirements for students considering short courses, completion of undergraduate or postgraduate programmes or further specialisation abroad. This service is aimed to provide guidance on requirements, paperwork, and deadlines associated with obtaining a valid student visa.

Additionally, we further offer personalised advice and support to students by helping them present a strong and compelling case for their visa application regardless of their unique circumstances or challenges that could impact their visa approval or delay their enrolment.
